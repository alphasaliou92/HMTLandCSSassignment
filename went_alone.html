He Went Alone To The Game

Even though none of the people he was thinking of were available, Jason managed to go alone at the basket ball game. He was able to have a good time and he was extremely satisfied. On his way out, he met a girl called Jenny with whom he became friend. They both found that they had a lot things in common.
